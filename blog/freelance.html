The Wonderfully Weird World of Freelancing
June 8, 2020
My time as a freelancer was short. Very short. About 1 year, in fact.
In that very short year, I ended up in 3 very different bizzare scenarios. So much so that I feel it's worthy of it's own writeup.
Freelancing can be fantastic. You get a lot of benefits, namely,
  • You are your own boss.
  • You can make your own schedule.
  • You can work from anywhere.

Client #1
Let's meet our first client. Since popular freelance sites like UpWork were, and still probably are, out of my league, I had to resort to finding listings on Craigslist and Kijiji.
After 15 minutes of searching, I stumbled across a suitable posting and reached out. The job was to do some simple web and mobile design for a small business.
We hop on the phone, talk about requirements, and a week later I sent over the final revision. I get paid the day after, and all is well! My first client, what a great feeling!
I know, wholesome, right? Just wait.
